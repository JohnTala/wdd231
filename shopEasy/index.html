EasyShop Website Plan

Site Name

Name: EasyShop

Reason: The name "EasyShop" represents the core goal of the platform—to provide an easy and convenient online shopping experience for users. It’s short, memorable, and clearly communicates the function of the website.

Optional domain: easyshop.store

Site Purpose

The purpose of EasyShop is to offer a user-friendly and accessible e-commerce platform where customers can browse and purchase products across multiple categories—such as fashion, electronics, home essentials, and beauty. The website provides a seamless experience with intuitive navigation, personalized recommendations, and convenient access from any device.

Scenarios

  • What are the best daily deals or trending items available right now?
  • How can I track my order or contact customer support?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2C3E50 – Used for headings, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons.

Accent Color: #E67E22 – Used for highlights, badges, and icons.

Typography

  • Roboto – Used for body text and paragraph content. Clean and easy to read.
  • Montserrat – Used for headings and major titles for a modern and bold look.
